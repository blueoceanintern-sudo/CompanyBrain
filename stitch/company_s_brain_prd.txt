# PRODUCT: Company's Brain
## Brand: BlueOcean (EQuest Education Group, Vietnam)
## Type: B2B Enterprise AI Knowledge Agent

### Core Functionality
- Staff ask plain-language questions.
- System searches uploaded company documents.
- Returns grounded cited answers.
- No hallucination: "I don't know" if confidence is low.

### Visual Identity
- Font: Be Vietnam Pro
- Colors: #eff6ff (sidebar), #ffffff (content), #2563eb (royal blue accent).
- Feel: Minimal, airy, whitespace-heavy (Vercel-style).
- Constraints: No gradients, no navy backgrounds, no decorative illustrations.

### Layout Shell
- Sidebar: Collapsed by default (56px), expands to 220px. Background #eff6ff.
- Top bar: 50px tall, white, title on left, status/avatar on right.
- Content: Desktop-first, generous padding.
